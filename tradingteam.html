Trading Team

Take a deep dive into the world of trading as a member of QFin's trading team.

About the role

  • Participate in a semester-long trading competition crafted to teach you about basic trading strategies.
  • Experience live mock trading through our newly-developed trading system.
  • Engage in weekly workshops presented on various topics.
  • Learn how to make good trading decisions.
  • Receive valuable preparation for an intern or grad application at a trading firm.
  • Meet like-minded people!

About you

  • An interest in the financial markets (no prior experience required).
  • Basic probability and mathematics knowledge (normal distribution, random sampling etc.).
  • Elementary programming skills preferred but not required (we mostly work in Python notebooks).
  • Interest in learning about Quantitative Finance (no prior experience required).

How you can join

We love to keep it simple here at QFin. All you need to do is answer a few questions on the linked google form and we'll be in contact with you for a casual face-to-face interview. Feel free to contact us at qfinuwa@gmail.com with any queries or related questions.
