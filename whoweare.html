Who We Are

Our Mission

QFin was founded in 2021 with three core objectives in mind:

  • To provide a platform for interested individuals to get practical experience in the field of algorithmic and statistical trading.
  • To demystify the quantitative finance industry and help prepare students for a career in the industry.
  • To create an inclusive environment for students with common interests to meet one another.

Our Success

  • Fostered a collaborative community with students from various universities across Western Australia.
  • Sponsored by five leading trading firms in the Asia-Pacific region.
  • Won Best New Club at the 2021 UWA Student Guild Societies Council Awards.
