About Us

The intersection of Mathematics, Finance and Programming.

QFin's aim is to provide students with an insight into the trading industry. Via our educational workshops, networking events and competitions, we act as a launchpad into the intern and graduate programs the industry has to offer.

Unlike many profressions, there's no university degree for trading. Instead, this industry seeks the highest talent in problem solving and quick decision making, marking it as one of the most selective and compeitive fields for STEM students.

Events

Networking

We host a variety of events with industry leaders to give our members the opportunity to familiarise themselves with these companies and network with traders and recruiters.

Game Theory

When trading in the markets, perceiving your competitor's actions is just as important as devising your own. Our competition events put our members' strategic thinking to the test.

Technology

Trading firms work closely with technology to analyse data and produce strategies. We provide our members with workshops to enhance these skills and events to showcase them.

Trading Team

Take a deep dive into the world of trading as a member of QFin's trading team.

As a trading team member you will...

  • Participate in a semester-long trading competition crafted to teach you about basic trading strategies.
  • Experience live mock trading through our newly-developed trading system.
  • Engage in weekly workshops presented on various topics.
  • Learn how to make good trading decisions.
  • Receive valuable preparation for an intern or grad application at a trading firm.
  • Meet like-minded people!
